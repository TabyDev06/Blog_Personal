About Me

Hello! My name is Farid Matos Villarroel, I'm 18 years old and currently studying Software Engineering at SENATI, a well-known technical institute in Peru. Ever since I started my journey in tech, I've been especially drawn to Front-End development and how code can be used to create visually impactful and user-friendly web interfaces.

My goal is to become a professional FullStack developer, but for now, I'm focusing on building a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, responsive design, accessibility, and UI/UX principles. I'm already confident working with Flexbox, semantic HTML, and media queries, and I'm currently learning advanced topics like CSS Grid, transitions, animations, and modern layout techniques.

This blog is a personal space where I share what I've built, showcase the skills I've learned, and track my progress as I grow. Each section reflects a stage of my learning, and I constantly update the site as I improve — both technically and visually.

Outside of coding, I'm a big fan of Cristiano Ronaldo, who inspires me with his discipline, perseverance, and work ethic — values I also apply to my career. I'm currently looking for internship opportunities where I can keep learning, contribute to real projects, and grow professionally.

Thanks for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my projects and reach out if you'd like to connect!
