About Me

Hello! My name is Farid Matos Villarroel, I'm 18 years old and currently studying Software Engineering at SENATI, a well-known technical institute in Peru. From the moment I discovered the world of technology, I’ve been especially passionate about Front-End development — fascinated by how code can shape visually engaging and user-friendly experiences on the web.

My long-term goal is to become a professional FullStack developer, but right now, I’m focused on mastering the fundamentals. I’m building a strong foundation in semantic HTML, modern CSS, responsive design, accessibility standards, and UI/UX principles. I'm confident working with Flexbox and media queries, and currently learning more advanced topics such as CSS Grid, transitions, animations, and modern layout techniques.

This blog serves as my personal space to showcase what I’ve built, reflect on the skills I’ve learned, and track my progress as I grow. Each section represents a stage in my learning journey, and I’m constantly updating the site to reflect my evolution — both technically and visually.

Outside of coding, I’m a huge fan of Cristiano Ronaldo, whose discipline, perseverance, and work ethic have deeply inspired me. I apply those same values to my career in tech. I’m currently seeking internship opportunities where I can keep learning, contribute to real-world projects, and grow as a developer.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my projects and reach out if you'd like to connect!
